Hi, I’m Giulia: welcome to my personal website!

After earning my master’s degree in Nuclear Physics, I discovered an exciting new passion for computer science, starting my journey into the world of computing and blockchain technologies.
I am a currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien), specializing in Security and Privacy under the guidance of Univ.Prof. Matteo Maffei and Dr. Zeta Avarikioti. Since 2024, I have also been working with the team at Common Prefix , where we provide expert blockchain consulting.

My research interests span into blockchain interoperability, scalability protocols, sparse and light clients, layer-2 solutions, and crypto-economic incentives. My goal is to advance both the security and efficiency of decentralized systems. You can download my CV here.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I’m also an avid bookworm with a love for stories across all genres. Dive into some of my all-time favorite reads here.
